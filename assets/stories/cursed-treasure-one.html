The Cursed Treasure


A long time ago there lived the greatest treasure hunter in the world. His name was Williamson Thomas. He was a great treasure hunter and a great inventor. One day he heard the rumour of a treasure in the mountains of Himalayas. But no one believed it was true. So he went to the mountains of the Himalayas to prove that the rumour was true. The day he went to prove the rumour was true was 1989 January 22nd. He reached the Himalayas on January 24th.but he had no idea it was cursed not only that he did not know the whereabouts of the treasure. He searched the map for days and days until one day he found it. He did not understand the map. So he took it to a map specialist. His name was Brian o Danielle. The map specialist told the directions to Williamson but he did not understand so he asked Brian to go with him. He had a condition. He had to be paid. Brian wanted 300 dollars for 24 hours. Williamson agreed to the deal and said we will start the journey tomorrow in the early dawn of light. At last they reached the place they were looking for on 26th January 1989. There was no sign of a treasure nearby. Brian lost his hope but Williamson said never lose hope. Nothing is impossible, he said with a sad face. As he looked to the trees he saw symbols. Both of them followed the symbols and they found the treasure. Inside the chest was the treasure .On top of it was written “whoever finds this treasure with a partner one of them would die because this is a cursed treasure”. They both got scared by reading this. Brian said we should leave it like it is. Williamson was eager to see what was inside and get rich. So Williamson opened the treasure and a snake bit him seeing that Brian ran away unharmed..
